Published Catalogs

I have published hundreds of multi-epoch radial and projected rotational velocites for ultracool dwarfs, and can be accessed via CDS.
The RVs and vsinis of 37 T dwarfs and literature RVs for local 172 late-M and L dwarfs (Hsu et al. 2021) can be accessed via the CDS J/ApJS/257/45.
The RVs and vsinis of 702 late-M and L dwarfs from SDSS/APOGEE (Hsu et al. 2024b) can be accessed via the CDS J/ApJS/274/40.
